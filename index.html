The Problem

Most teams don't fail from lack of effort. They fail against invisible friction.

When change initiatives stall, communication breaks down, or talented people leave — the instinct is to work harder, hire better, or restructure. But the real culprit is almost always the Friction Tax: the psychological and systemic hurdles silently draining energy, eroding trust, and stalling progress.

What makes our approach different: we give individuals and leaders the tools, frameworks, and responsibility to identify and solve their own workplace frictions — not just top-down mandates, but real ownership from every level of the organization.

Founder · Lead Facilitator

Dr. Ryan Darby

Behavioral Scientist & Leadership Advisor

With a Ph.D. in Experimental Psychology, Dr. Ryan specializes in the "Human Why" of performance — moving beyond mechanical fixes to address the deep psychological patterns that limit individuals and teams.

He is the primary facilitator for all Work Better Co. programs, bringing a rare combination of research rigor and practical immediacy. His work helps high-performers identify and remove invisible hurdles so they can reach their natural best — not by working harder, but by removing what's in the way.

Dr. Darby has published peer-reviewed research in behavioral and social psychology, with work appearing in Cambridge University Press, Basic and Applied Social Psychology, Memory & Cognition, and Psychosomatic Medicine.
